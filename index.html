Biography

Forest genetic improvement is a time-consuming and labor-intensive work, and at present, the improvement level of the main timber species in China lags behind that of developed countries. The use of state-of-the-art technology can speed up the improvement of economic traits and, combined with reasonable cultivation and management programs, can satisfy people’s needs for forest products to the maximum. The core purpose of forest genetic improvement is to maximize genetic gain. The approach is to shorten the breeding cycle, improve the choice of intensity and improve the accuracy of genetic information estimates. My PhD research work focused on the establishment of pedigree reconstruction, spatial and competition analysis based genetic improvement system. Mixed linear model is a common tool in data processing. I am interested in the development and application of the model. In addition, genomic selection is currently a hot area in animal and plant genetic improvement and has many advantages over conventional selection strategies. How to introduce this method and optimize the domestic forest genetic improvement project in China is also of interest to me.

Mobile visual clothing search

A mobile visual clothing search system is presented whereby a smart phone user can either choose a social networking image or capture a new photo of a person wearing clothing of interest and search for similar clothing in a large cloud-based ecommerce database. The phone’s GPS location is used to re-rank results by retail store location, to inform the user of local stores where similar clothing items can be tried on.
GA Cushen, MS Nixon
